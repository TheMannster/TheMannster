## What I'm about

I'm **slowly learning** in **Lua** and **FiveM** scripting. I'm not pretending to be a wizard — I'm still in the *figure-it-out, break-it, fix-it* phase. That's the fun part.

I care about **clean, maintainable resource code** and scripts people can **read, customize, and repair** without begging the original author for support or fixing their resource.

---

## Why I open-source almost everything

Too many FiveM resources go **abandoned** behind paywalls or “encrypted” drops. Players and server owners get left holding a bag they **can't fix or extend**.

My default plan: **ship open source** so the community can:

- **Fork and improve** instead of starting from zero  
- **Learn** from real production patterns (messy commits and all)  
- **Keep servers running** when life gets in the way  

If something I publish helps your server, a star or a PR back is plenty to pay it forward.
